本頁符合 Google Play 資料安全政策的帳戶刪除要求，適用於花蓮計程車叫車服務 GoGoCha 的乘客端與司機端使用者。

如何要求刪除帳戶

您可以透過以下方式要求刪除 GoGoCha 帳戶與關聯資料：

  1. 以註冊本 App 的手機門號寄信至 26416387.re@gmail.com
    📧 點此寄出刪除申請
  2. 信件主旨請寫：GoGoCha 帳戶刪除申請
  3. 內文請提供：
    • 您註冊時使用的台灣手機門號（如 09xx-xxx-xxx）
    • 您的角色：乘客或司機
    • 您的暱稱（供核對）
  4. 我們會在 7 個工作天內 確認您的身份並完成刪除，完成後寄送確認信給您。

如果您只想刪除特定資料（例如位置歷史紀錄、大頭照）但保留帳戶，請同樣來信說明要求，我們會在 7 個工作天內處理。

Delete your GoGoCha Account (English)

For users requesting account deletion in English.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from any address to 26416387.re@gmail.com with subject GoGoCha Account Deletion Request.
  2. Include: the Taiwan phone number you registered with, your role (passenger or driver), and your display name for verification.
  3. We delete your account and personal data within 7 business days and send a confirmation email.

What gets deleted immediately

Phone number, display name, Firebase Auth account, FCM token, location history, uploaded photos, vehicle plate (drivers).

What we retain (legal obligations)

  • Order transaction records — retained for 2 years (Taiwan tax law), de-identified before retention.
  • Dispute / complaint messages — retained for 1 year after case closure.

Partial deletion

You may request deletion of specific data (location history, photos, etc.) without deleting your account. Email us with details.
